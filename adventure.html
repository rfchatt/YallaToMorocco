Sahara adventure

Sahara adventures in Morocco offer an unforgettable journey into one of the most breathtaking landscapes on Earth. Travelers can explore the golden sand dunes of Merzouga or Zagora, ride camels under the vast desert sky, and witness magical sunsets that paint the horizon in shades of orange and pink. Nights in the Sahara are equally mesmerizing, with traditional Berber camps, warm hospitality, and the chance to enjoy music and stories around a campfire beneath a sky full of stars. Whether it's sandboarding down towering dunes or simply soaking in the peace of the desert, a Sahara adventure in Morocco is an experience that blends natural beauty, cultural richness, and timeless memories.

Hiking Mount Toubkal

Hiking Mount Toubkal in Morocco is a thrilling adventure for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Standing at 4,167 meters, it is the highest peak in North Africa, offering breathtaking views of the Atlas Mountains and beyond. The trek usually starts from the village of Imlil, winding through rugged trails, Berber villages, and scenic valleys. Along the way, hikers experience the region's rich culture and warm hospitality. Reaching the summit is challenging but incredibly rewarding, with panoramic landscapes stretching as far as the Sahara on a clear day. Whether in summer's clear skies or winter's snow-covered peaks, hiking Toubkal is an unforgettable Moroccan adventure.

Hot Air Balloon Tour

Why not enjoy this beautiful paradise in the bird's eye view floating over the thick dense forests, lush paddy fields and famous landmarks from the sky! Sign up for a balloon ride in your holiday in Sri Lanka and you'll be delighted by the surprises waiting for you. November to May, the low wind season is the best time for hot air ballooning in Sri Lanka although March to April offers the best favourable weather for the activity.
Kandalama lake in Dambulla is where you should head to in early in the morning for an air ballooning session. The ride starts before the dawn promising you a mind-blowing sunrise and breathtaking scenery of the island from above.

Skiing in Ifrane

Experience the thrill of skiing in the heart of Morocco's Middle Atlas Mountains with a trip to Ifrane, often referred to as "Little Switzerland"
due to its alpine-style architecture and snowy winters. Ifrane is home to the Michlifen Ski Resort, which offers a variety of slopes suitable for both beginners and experienced skiers.
The resort features well-maintained pistes, ski rentals, and professional instructors to help you make the most of your skiing adventure. Beyond skiing, Ifrane's picturesque landscapes, charming chalets, and serene atmosphere provide a perfect backdrop for a winter getaway. Whether you're carving down the slopes or enjoying a cozy evening
by the fire, skiing in Ifrane promises an unforgettable Moroccan experience.
